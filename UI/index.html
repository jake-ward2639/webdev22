How to Use

To submitt a sighting you must submit a name and the condition the animal was found in, you may also add additional notes if you wish but this is not mandatory.

We automatically use your current location when submitting a sighting. While you can fill out your information offline, an internet connection and your location are required to submit data. As such if you regain internet connection away from the sighting, you may adjust the submitted location by dragging and dropping the marker seen on our map.

Only one file can be chosen to be submitted and we only accept image files (e.g. jpg and png). On mobile you may chose an image from gallery or take a photo to submit.

After clicking the submit button your submission will be sent to our team for analysis. Thank you for your support.
